Computer Netwroks and The Internet - Introduction

Where is your Device on the Internet?

Computer Networks refer to interconnected devices that share resources and exchange data using communication protocols.

A network is interconnection between devices. For example suppose in your home, 5 devices are connected to your router, so this interconnection between 5 computers is called a Local Area Network of 5 devices.

The Internet is a global network of networks that enables billions of devices to communicate and access information.

On the internet, your device is identified by an address called IP address. You are connected to the Internet, means your device has an ip address.
